grainfield is born from two ideas: the grain - the smallest particle of sound in granular synthesis, and the field - the vast landscape of audio. We’re a creative team of youths that specialise in contemporary music production, sound design and mix engineering. Our vision is to innovate something revolutionary in the field of sound and music.

Our coursework emphasizes advanced mixing techniques and critical listening. We practice sound design, composition, and music production, combining technical expertise with artistic creativity. Passionate about crafting immersive audio experiences, we aim to push sonic boundaries in animation, film, and the music industry - blending sound design, composition, and production to create impactful auditory narratives.

about

I’m Haris, a final year Audio Production student at LASALLE College of the Arts, Singapore, and the founder of Grainfield.

My journey in audio spans songwriting, composing and engineering, but what drives me most is the art of sound design. It’s allows me to get crafty, and focus on creative storytelling be it music or effects, despite the technicalities. I have chosen to specialise in sound design for my final year project as well as taken on gigs outside of school to broaden my experience.

As a musician, I’m on a mission to push boundaries and innovate a sub-genre which I call “hyperfolk”. Fusing native folk instruments from cultures around the world with the high-energy, experimental textures of hyperpop, I hope to create music that defies theory. Alongside this, I compose original music and songs that reflect my belief that genre is a starting point, not a limitation.

Grainfield is the starting point for all of this - a space where I experiment during the process of creation to reach my ultimate goal: to make our projects resonate.
